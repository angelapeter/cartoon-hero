Rapunzel from Tangled

About Rapunzel from Tangled

Rapunzel is a long-haired princess who has magic in her hair. She is shut out from living an ordinary life by a witch who uses Rapunzel's hair to look youthful

Rapunzel's Characteristics

  1. She has long hair
  2. Her hair has magical powers
  3. She was born a princess, but kidnapped by an evil witch

Rapunzel and her Friends
